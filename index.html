FAQs

Questions from couples and hosts

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend 2–3 weeks for custom wedding cakes. For express celebrations, call us and we’ll do our best to accommodate.

Do you offer tastings?

Yes. Tastings are available by appointment and include our signature vanilla, chocolate, and pistachio selections.

Can the cake match my wedding palette?

Absolutely. We tailor finishes, sugar flowers, and gilded details to create a seamless look for your event.
